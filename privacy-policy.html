Privacy Policy - Longan Detection App

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

This privacy policy explains how the Longan Detection App collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Longan Detection App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This app is designed to help users classify longan leaf images using machine learning technology. We respect your privacy and handle your data responsibly.

2. Permissions Required

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

Camera Permission

  • Purpose: To allow you to take pictures of longan leaves directly within the app
  • Usage: Used exclusively for capturing images of longan leaves for classification purposes
  • Note: The camera is only activated when you choose to take a photo within the app

Read External Storage / Read Media Images

  • Purpose: To allow you to select existing images from your device's gallery or storage
  • Usage: Used to access and retrieve longan leaf images you've previously saved on your device
  • Note: We only access images that you explicitly select for classification

3. What Data We Collect

The Longan Detection App collects only the following information:

  • Images: Photos of longan leaves that you capture using the camera or select from your device storage
  • Classification Results: The analysis results generated by our machine learning model

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • Any images other than those you explicitly provide for classification

4. How We Use Your Data

The images you provide through the app are used solely for the following purposes:

  • Classification: To analyze longan leaves and provide disease or health status detection
  • Image Storage: Images are temporarily stored in Google Cloud Storage for processing and retrieval
  • Service Improvement: To maintain and improve the accuracy of our classification model (only with your consent)

Your email is use solely for this purpose:

  • User authentication: To authenticate user using our app, we using authentication service of Google Firebase, your email and/or in-app password just for security purpose and no data is stored

Important: All images taken or selected through this app are used EXCLUSIVELY for longan leaf classification purposes. We do NOT use your images for:

  • Commercial purposes
  • Sale or distribution to third parties
  • Unauthorized data mining
  • Hacking or malicious activities
  • Marketing or advertising

5. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Storage Platform: Images are stored in Google Cloud Storage, a secure and reliable cloud infrastructure
  • Purpose: Storage is used for temporary retrieval and processing of images for classification
  • Access Control: Access to stored images is restricted to authorized app functions only
  • Encryption: Data transmission between your device and our servers is encrypted
  • Retention: Images may be retained for the duration necessary to provide the service and may be deleted upon request

6. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Cloud Storage: Used for secure image storage and retrieval. Google's privacy policies also apply to data stored on their platform. You can review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view the images you've submitted through the app
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your images at any time
  • Opt-Out: You can choose not to use the app if you don't agree with this policy
  • Permission Control: You can revoke camera and storage permissions through your device settings at any time

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or distribute your images or data to any third parties for commercial, marketing, or any other purposes. Your images are used exclusively within the app for classification purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general use in agricultural and educational contexts.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
